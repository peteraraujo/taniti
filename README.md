# Taniti Tourism Website

A university UI/UX project centered around designing a tourism website for the fictional island of Taniti. The project focuses on the complete UX lifecycle, from user research and information architecture to wireframing, prototyping, accessibility, and responsive design.

### About Taniti

Taniti is a small tropical island in the Pacific known for its beaches, rainforest, volcano, local culture, and growing tourism industry. Visitors can explore attractions such as:

* Beaches and sightseeing tours
* Rainforest hikes and zip-lining
* Snorkeling and fishing tours
* Museums, pubs, art galleries, and nightlife
* Merriton Landing entertainment district

The island also offers restaurants, lodging, transportation services, and tourist-friendly amenities.

### Project Goals

* Design an intuitive tourism experience
* Improve discoverability of attractions and services
* Create accessible and responsive UI designs
* Apply UX research and usability principles
* Practice the end-to-end UI/UX process

### Features

* Destination and activity browsing
* Lodging and restaurant information
* Transportation and travel details
* Frequently requested visitor information
* Responsive layouts for desktop and mobile

### UX Process

* User research
* Personas and user flows
* Information architecture
* Wireframes and mockups
* High-fidelity prototypes
* Usability evaluation
