Explore Taniti’s Adventures

Taniti is packed with fun things to do, from chilling on beautiful beaches to hiking through rainforests or dancing the night away. Whether you’re into adventure, culture, or family-friendly activities, there’s something here for everyone. Check out the options below to plan your perfect trip!

Snorkeling in Yellow Leaf Bay

Jump into the clear waters of Yellow Leaf Bay to see colorful fish and coral reefs up close. Guided tours make it easy for beginners or pros to enjoy this underwater adventure. It’s a must-do for ocean lovers!

  • Duration: 2–3 hours
  • Location: Yellow Leaf Bay
  • Equipment: Masks and fins included

Rainforest Zip-Lining

Fly through the treetops in Merriton Landing for an exciting adventure with amazing views. Zip-lining here lets you see Taniti’s rainforest from a whole new angle. Morning trips are best to beat the heat!

  • Height: Up to 100 feet
  • Safety: Certified guides and harnesses
  • Best Time: Morning for cooler weather

Volcano Hike

Hike up Taniti’s active volcano with a local guide to learn about its history and geology. The trail offers epic views of the island, perfect for adventurers. It’s a bit challenging but totally worth it!

  • Difficulty: Moderate to challenging
  • Duration: 4–6 hours
  • Includes: Guide and water

Cultural Experiences

Check out Taniti City’s history museum to see cool artifacts or wander through art galleries showcasing local talent. These spots dive deep into the island’s indigenous culture and traditions. They’re great for a laid-back, cultural day out.

  • Museum Hours: 10 a.m.–5 p.m., closed Mondays
  • Galleries: Taniti City
  • Highlights: Traditional crafts

Island Tours

Take a boat or bus tour to see Taniti’s stunning beaches, rainforests, and unique architecture. These guided trips cover top spots like Yellow Leaf Bay and give you fun facts about the island. Perfect for soaking in the scenery!

  • Duration: 3–5 hours
  • Locations: Taniti City, Yellow Leaf Bay
  • Includes: Expert guide

Chartered Fishing Tours

Head out on a fishing boat from Yellow Leaf Bay’s harbor for a fun day catching local fish. These tours are great for beginners or seasoned anglers looking to reel in a big one. All gear is provided for an easy trip.

  • Duration: 4–6 hours
  • Location: Yellow Leaf Bay harbor
  • Equipment: Rods and bait included

Nightlife & Entertainment

Dance the night away at Taniti City’s new club or sip craft beers at the local microbrewery. Cozy pubs offer a chill vibe with live music and island drinks. It’s the perfect way to unwind after a day of exploring!

  • Hours: 6 p.m.–midnight
  • Location: Taniti City
  • Highlights: Live music, local brews

Family Fun

Bring the kids to Merriton Landing’s arcade for games or hit the bowling alley for some friendly competition. Taniti City’s movie theater is great for catching a new film. These spots are fun for all ages!

  • Hours: Noon–10 p.m. daily
  • Location: Merriton Landing, Taniti City
  • Suitable For: All ages

Helicopter Rides

Fly over Taniti for jaw-dropping views of its beaches, rainforests, and volcano. These guided aerial tours are a thrilling way to see the island’s beauty from above. Perfect for a special adventure!

  • Duration: 30–60 minutes
  • Departure: Near Taniti City airport
  • Includes: Aerial tour guide

Coming Soon: Golf Course

A new nine-hole golf course near Merriton Landing is set to open next year. It’s perfect for golfers of all levels looking for a relaxing day with scenic views. Stay tuned for updates!

  • Opening: Expected mid-2026
  • Features: Clubhouse and rentals
  • Location: Near Yellow Leaf Bay
