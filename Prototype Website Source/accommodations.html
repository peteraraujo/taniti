Find Your Perfect Stay in Taniti

Choose from a variety of lodging options, from an affordable hostel to a luxurious four-star resort, with a growing number of charming bed & breakfasts. All accommodations are strictly regulated by the Tanitian government for quality.

Explore Taniti’s Accommodations

Discover the perfect place to stay, whether in vibrant Taniti City or near the serene Yellow Leaf Bay.

  • Hostel: Budget-friendly option with shared spaces in Taniti City, ideal for solo travelers.
  • Family-Owned Hotels: Cozy stays in Taniti City, offering a warm, local experience.
  • Bed & Breakfasts: Growing in number, these provide intimate settings with personalized hospitality.
  • Beach Houses & Condos: Private retreats near Yellow Leaf Bay’s sandy shores, perfect for relaxation.
  • Four-Star Resort: Luxurious amenities near Merriton Landing for a premium getaway.
