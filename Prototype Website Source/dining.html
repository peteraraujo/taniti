Savor Taniti’s Flavors

Taniti’s dining scene has something for everyone, from fresh island seafood to juicy burgers and spicy Pan-Asian dishes. You can also grab groceries at local stores for a home-cooked meal. Note that alcohol sales stop from midnight to 9 a.m., and some spots may close during holidays, listed on our About page.

Local Cuisine

  • Family-Friendly
  • Gluten-Free
  • Taniti City

Enjoy fresh fish and rice at five cozy spots in Taniti City. These restaurants serve up island flavors with locally sourced seafood and tropical twists. Perfect for a taste of Tanitian tradition!

American-Style

  • Family-Friendly
  • Vegetarian
  • Taniti City

Craving comfort food? Three American-style restaurants offer burgers, sandwiches, and desserts that feel like home. Great for kids and adults looking for a familiar meal.

Pan-Asian

  • Vegetarian
  • Gluten-Free
  • Taniti City

Two Pan-Asian spots in Taniti City mix Thai, Japanese, and Chinese flavors with island ingredients. Think spicy noodle bowls and fresh sushi rolls. A fun pick for bold foodies!

Grocery Stores

  • Vegetarian
  • Gluten-Free
  • Family-Friendly

Stock up at two supermarkets or two smaller grocery stores in Taniti City for fresh produce and essentials. A 24-hour convenience store is open for late-night snacks. Ideal for self-catering in beach houses or condos!
